Research

Current and past research across epidemiology, data science, clinical medicine, and infectious disease.

Sept 2024 – Present
Cancer Epidemiology Health Disparities OMOP CDM

UC Health Data Warehouse & CHEER Lab

UC San Diego Health · Moores Cancer Center · La Jolla, CA

Center for Health Equity Education & Research (CHEER)

Conduct large-scale epidemiological research using the OMOP Common Data Model across multi-institutional UC Health datasets encompassing 15M+ patient records. Key projects include:

  • Cancer Survivorship & Psychiatric Outcomes: Designed and executed a multi-center retrospective cohort study examining the incidence and timing of new-onset depression and anxiety following cancer diagnosis, with insurance type as primary exposure and Charlson Comorbidity Index adjustment. Developed complex SQL pipelines and Python analysis workflows in Databricks for data extraction, transformation, survival analysis, and multivariable regression modeling.
  • Prostate Cancer Screening Disparities: Authored a paper on sociodemographic disparities in prostate cancer screening using the All of Us Research Program database, identifying predictors of lower PSA testing rates including high Area Deprivation Index, healthcare discrimination, rural access delays, and limited health literacy.
  • Wastewater-Based Cancer Epidemiology: Conducting pre-pilot research on the association between socio-environmental determinants and wastewater-based detection of oncogenic microorganisms in Imperial Valley, CA. Utilizing WBE and shotgun metagenomic sequencing to analyze viral, bacterial, and parasitic pathogens linked to infection-related cancers. Integrating CDC's Environmental Justice Index to evaluate correlations between microbial diversity and community-level vulnerability.
Feb 2025 – Present
Otolaryngology Facial Nerve Pediatrics

Qian Lab – Pediatric Otolaryngology

UCSD School of Medicine · Dept. of Pediatrics & Otolaryngology · La Jolla, CA

  • Published research on clinical trial publication rates in otolaryngology and otitis media, stratified by funder type, intervention type, and industry sponsorship (Otolaryngology–Head and Neck Surgery).
  • Leading systematic reviews and meta-analyses on AI-based binary classification for otitis media diagnosis.
  • Analyzed 43,000+ patient records for Bell's palsy and facial nerve disorder outcomes across multiple etiologies using UCHDW data.
  • Conducted multi-site analysis of occult lesion diagnosis rates among 25,000 patients with Bell's palsy across a de-identified multi-institutional cohort.
  • Led research on pediatric tonsillectomy outcomes including surgical setting comparisons (clean vs. sterile) and pre-operative weight gain as a predictor of failure in pediatric obstructive sleep apnea.
  • Analyzed pediatric otolaryngology fellowship training trends and career outcomes spanning 2015–2025.
  • Investigated facial reanimation patterns after total parotidectomy with nerve sacrifice using multi-center retrospective data.
  • Evaluated static reconstruction procedure utilization among head and neck cancer patients with facial palsy in the immunotherapy era.
